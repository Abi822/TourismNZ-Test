Destination Informaton!

The Hobbiton:

The Hobbiton location is kown as the Shire with a village of 44 hobbits holes dug into a hillside which is surrounded by hobbit gardens and orchards. It was built in December 2012 in the Green Dragon Inn and is used for movie sets, like the hobbiton.

Auckland Sky Tower:

Auckland Sky Tower is an observation and telecommunications tower located at the corner of V ictoria and Federal Streets in the Auckland CBD. The tower is known as one of the biggest towers in New Zealand, as its 1,076 ft tall with 60 levels.

Mount of Maunganui:

Mount Maunganui or known as Mauao, is an extinct volcanic cone. It is considered very important by maori culture filled with lots of mythology and historical value as now days used for scenic walks, jogging and even hang gliding

Bath House Museum:

The Rotorua Museum is located in the Government Gardens and is local museum and art gallery located, the museum is housed in the old Bath House building which was opened in 1908. It is known as the first major investment in the New Zealand tourism by the government.

Skyline Gondola:

The skyline gondola in queenstown can go upto 450 meters above Lake Wakatipu, it is one of the steepest cable car lift in the Southern Hemisphere and in New Zealand.

Huka Falls:

Huka Falls are a set of waterfalls on the Waikato River that drains Lake Taupo, a few 100 metres from Huka falls is the Waikato River which narrows from around 100 metres across into a canyon only 15 metres across. Most of the water comes from melting of the ice on mountains near bay and downstream rivers with a average flow rage of 200,000 liters a second
