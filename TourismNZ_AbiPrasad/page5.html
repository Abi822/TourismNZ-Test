    Venice Information

    Venice is the capital of Veneto region, it lays in the northern Italy. Venice contains 118 small islands in a lagoon in the Adriatic Sea, people travel across the island using boats and kayaks. There are very little to no roads but there over are 400 bridges separated by canals, all linked together surrounding Venice from island to island.

    Venice is renowned for the beauty of their settings, their architecture, and artwork as it known as the City of Water. Venice is more then 260,000 and is still growing. The Center of Venice is the Piazza San Marco which is known as one of the finest squares in Europe.
